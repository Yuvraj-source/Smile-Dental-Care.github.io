New Patient Information

Forms and Referrals

  • Complete your new patient forms.
  • If you are being referred, have your current provider complete a referral form.
  • Have any x-rays or records transferred to us by your previous provider.

Check-In and Appointment

  • Plan to arrive at least 10 min early for check-in process.
  • Expect appointment to last 1 hour.

During your first visit to the dentist you can expect

  • A comprehensive dental exam
  • X-rays to check for cavities and other dental problems
  • A cleaning to remove plaque and tartar buildup
